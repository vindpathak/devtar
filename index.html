A modern myth — war within, war without.

When ancient legend finds a soldier's veins

Major Dev Rawat carries an ancient demonic element in his veins. The old war between Asuras and Devas spills into the human world — and Dev must fight on three fronts: for his country, for humanity, and for the universe itself.

Synopsis

The war that began between Asuras and Devas has now reached within humans. Major Dev Rawat's battles are not just external: the demonic element inside him pulls at his choices, his loyalties, his sense of self. As a fearsome Asura returns and an external enemy threatens to bring his country to its knees, Dev becomes the only hope — a guardian whose victory may cost him everything.
