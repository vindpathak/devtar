About Devtar

Devtar is not just a hero — he is a symbol of inner strength, sacrifice, and hope. The story blends myth and modernity, designed to resonate with both children and young adults, while offering the excitement of an international action-thriller.

About DEVTAR Saga

A universe is awakening… Where gods, demons, and soldiers walk the same battlefield. Three realms. One destiny. The Asuras — banished thousands of years ago — are returning. The Earth trembles, the forgotten realm stirs, and a hidden world still guards secrets no human has seen. This is not just one story. It is the beginning of a mythic universe, an epic saga told through a series of novels — where Hindu mythology blends with modern warfare, and ancient legends shape the battles of today. The war for tomorrow has already begun.
